📜 The Quest to Contact Me

So... you seek an audience with the great Dylan? A noble goal, indeed. But before you can earn this sacred knowledge, you must prove yourself worthy. The path will be treacherous, the riddles unfathomable, and your WiFi... probably unstable.

⚔️ The Trials You Must Complete:

  1. 🦄 Find the last living unicorn and convince it to let you ride it to the summit of Mount Ethernet.
  2. 🔱 Retrieve the legendary Ethernet Cable of Destiny buried deep beneath the ruins of Windows Vista.
  3. 🐉 Defeat the Firewall Dragon that guards the sacred inbox.
  4. 🎸 Perform "Through the Fire and Flames" on expert mode in Guitar Hero while blindfolded.
  5. 🚀 Send a carrier pigeon with a handwritten letter encoded in Morse code—but only during a solar eclipse.

📡 Alternative Methods (For Mere Mortals)

If you lack the courage for the sacred trials above, you may attempt these lesser methods (though success is not guaranteed):

  • 📧 Whisper your message to the wind and hope it reaches the Gmail gods.
  • 📱 Send a text, but only if Mercury is in retrograde.
  • 📦 Leave a package at your doorstep. If I ever become a wandering monk, I might pick it up.
  • 📡 Try shouting really loud. Maybe, just maybe, I'll hear you.

🏆 Should You Succeed...

If, against all odds, you complete this impossible quest, a portal shall open. Through it, you may glimpse the ultimate reward: a reply from Dylan Worth. But beware! Many have tried, and none have returned... except that one guy, but we don't talk about him.

Good luck, brave traveler. You shall need it. 🏹
