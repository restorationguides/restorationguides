About This Site

WRT Study Hub was created by Dylan Worth to help professionals studying for the Water Restoration Technician (WRT) certification. This site offers interactive tools, study guides, and quizzes to improve knowledge of moisture monitoring, psychrometrics, drying strategies, and the IICRC S500 standards.
