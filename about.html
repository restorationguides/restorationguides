📖 About This Site

The Restoration Hub was created by Dylan Worth to help professionals studying for the Water Restoration Technician (WRT) certification. This site offers interactive tools, study guides, and quizzes to improve knowledge of moisture monitoring, psychrometry, drying strategies, and the IICRC S500 standards.

🧑‍🏭 About Dylan Worth

Dylan is an experienced mold remediation specialist and water damage expert, working toward becoming a Mold Assessor. Passionate about open-source technology, leadership, and helping others grow, he built this site as a free study resource for WRT students and future professionals.

📌 Why This Site?

  • 📚 A free study guide covering all major WRT topics.
  • 🧠 An interactive psychrometric chart for real-world calculations.
  • ✅ A quiz section to test your knowledge before the WRT exam.
